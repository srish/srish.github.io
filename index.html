Srishti Sethi SrishAkaTux

Hi! Born and brought up in a desert town of Rajasthan, India, I'm currently a Masters student at MIT Media Lab with Lifelong Kindergarten research group advised by Mitchel Resnick. With Philipp Schmidt, I'm working on open and online learning projects: Unhangout, Learning Creative Learning and Making Learning Work.

Before coming to the Media Lab, I was a software developer at VCreateLogic in Bangalore. While in undergrad, I was involved with an open source community, GNOME. As a Student Contractor at Google, I developed an engaging user-experience by extending GCompris to kids to help them explore a variety of activities theming braille.

Academics focus aside, I harbor a deep interest for music and ping-pong. I love to blog about my experiences with life, technology and travel. To allure myself at times, I read spiritual books, bike around, do photography and make masala chai for friends.
