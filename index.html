Hey there, I am Srishti! I currently work as a Developer Advocate in the Technical Collaboration team at the Wikimedia Foundation. I help lead the organization's efforts to engage volunteer developers in Wikimedia software projects and to grow the Wikimedia technical community.

I graduated with Masters degree in Media Arts and Sciences from the Lifelong Kindergarten research group at the MIT Media Lab. While at the Lab, I helped develop Unhangout, a platform for running large-scale unconferences online.

Outside of work, I like to spend time with friends and family, go on bike trips with Schweenie , play ping-pong, and hunt for the best chai serving restaurants around.
