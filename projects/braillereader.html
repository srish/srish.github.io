Mobile Braille Tutor for Blinds

Mobile braille tutor for Blinds targeting Nokia and android smart phones acts as a learning aid for visually impaired kids. This application consists of two levels .The first level allows a kid to locate braille dots calibrated on each corner of the screen. An audio feedback is given correspondingly in both the levels. In the second level user is required to produce a braille character by generating different combination of dots pressed or released. For instance , the app will read aloud C dot one four . So , a combination of (1,4) will result in letter C.
