Media Lab Virtual Visit

Media Lab Virtual Visit is intended to open up the doors of the Media Lab to people from all around the world. So far, I have organized three virtual visits on the Unhangout platform, a new way of running large-scale unconferences on the web that was developed at the Media Lab. It is an opportunity for students or potential collaborators to talk with current researchers at the Lab, learn about their work and share ideas. During the event, after a short welcome webinar, we encourage people to join a breakout group of their choice. Each conversation in the breakout group is hosted by current Media Lab students who talk about their research, the philosophy of their groups, and what goes on at the lab in general.

For more information, visit the event website.
