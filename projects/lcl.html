Learning Creative Learning

Learning Creative Learning is the Media Lab version of Massive Open Online course that encourages like-minded educators and learners from all around the world to share their ideas on creative learning. This course is designed and led by Mitch Resnick, Philipp Schmidt, and Natalie Rusk. In the spring of 2014, I participated in some of the activities of this course, and helped in setting up the course website, discussion forum (powered on discourse platform) and in organizing the online seminars on the Unhangout platform.
