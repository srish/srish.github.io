Braille Concept on GCompris

GCompris is a highly educational software suite comprising of numerous activities for children aged 2 to 10. Braille concept on GCompris is to let kids explore a variety of interactive activities theming braille. I worked on this project as a part of Google Summer of Code program. These activities are developed using Python, GTK and PyGoocanvas.
